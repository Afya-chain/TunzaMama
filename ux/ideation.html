3. SCAMPER Technique

(Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to another use, Eliminate, Reverse)

Substitute

  • Replace paper records with NFTs
  • Switch facility visits with virtual consultations
  • Transform physical tokens to digital rewards

Combine

  • Merge traditional and modern medicine tracking
  • Unite community transport with emergency response
  • Blend AI diagnostics with human expertise

Adapt

  • Modify urban solutions for rural contexts
  • Adjust blockchain for offline use
  • Transform social features for health support

Modify

  • Enhance emergency response with AI
  • Amplify community involvement with tokens
  • Magnify impact through data analytics

Put to another use

  • Repurpose social networks for health support
  • Convert gaming mechanics for health tracking
  • Transform educational content into community resources

Eliminate

  • Remove connectivity dependencies
  • Reduce technical complexity
  • Minimize data entry burden

Reverse/Rearrange

  • Flip provider-patient dynamic
  • Invert data flow
  • Reorganize care pathways

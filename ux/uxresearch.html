MamaAI Component-Focused UX Research Plan

1. User Research Phase

Home Screen Research

Main Questions:

  • Walk me through how you track your pregnancy progress currently
  • In an emergency, what steps do you take to get help?
  • How do you prefer to receive daily health information?

Observation Tasks:

  • Watch users organize pregnancy-related information
  • Observe emergency response behaviors
  • Monitor daily health information consumption

Health Assessment Research

Main Questions:

  • How do you currently decide whether to visit a health facility?
  • What symptoms make you most concerned during pregnancy?
  • How do you share health concerns with healthcare providers?

Observation Tasks:

  • Watch users describe symptoms
  • Observe decision-making process for seeking care
  • Monitor interaction with existing health apps

Education Hub Research

Main Questions:

  • What languages do you prefer for health information?
  • How do you currently learn about pregnancy and childcare?
  • What motivates you to complete educational content?

Observation Tasks:

  • Observe users interacting with educational content
  • Monitor language preferences and switching
  • Watch quiz/game interaction patterns

Care Connector Research

Main Questions:

  • How do you currently find and choose healthcare facilities?
  • What challenges do you face in scheduling appointments?
  • What would make you comfortable with virtual consultations?

Observation Tasks:

  • Watch facility selection process
  • Observe appointment booking behaviors
  • Monitor virtual consultation comfort levels

Community Support Research

Main Questions:

  • How do you connect with other pregnant women?
  • What role do local women's groups play in your pregnancy?
  • What type of support do you seek from the community?

Observation Tasks:

  • Observe community interaction patterns
  • Monitor support-seeking behaviors
  • Watch group participation dynamics

Health Worker Portal Research

Main Questions:

  • What information do you need to make care decisions?
  • How do you currently manage patient records?
  • What challenges do you face in emergency situations?

Observation Tasks:

  • Watch health worker decision-making process
  • Observe record management practices
  • Monitor emergency response protocols

Data Analytics Dashboard Research

Main Questions:

  • What metrics are most important for maternal health monitoring?
  • How do you currently track supply chain issues?
  • What performance indicators matter most?

Observation Tasks:

  • Watch data analysis workflows
  • Observe supply chain management practices
  • Monitor performance tracking methods
